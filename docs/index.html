Whats this about...?

In the early 2000s, I (Vergel) was a fan of Techno, but also working as a web developer building for the web. And from that intersection of nerdy tech and pattern based live performance techno, I made this project.

Back at it again in 2026, here we are sharing our collective time, thoughts, and ideas about technology, creativity, and the future. I hope you enjoy the vibes. And if you want to keep the conversation going ... Jump on Youtube and join in on the comments.
